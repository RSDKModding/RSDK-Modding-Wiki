Hedgehog Engine 2 (also referred to as HE2) is a game engine created by Sonic Team. The engine is a successor to the original Hedgehog Engine. The engine was created for Sonic Forces, and has been used in several other SEGA games since, such as Mario & Sonic at the Tokyo 2020 Olympic Games, Puyo Puyo Tetris 2, and [Sonic Origins](README.md).

## Sonic Origins

In addition to Hedgehog Engine 2, [Sonic Origins](README.md) also incorporates [Retro Engine v5U](/RSDKv5) as a backend to run the four playable remasters in the collection.
